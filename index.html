Our Story

Tamu Thakali Ghar - Kitchen & Bar brings authentic Himalayan flavors to your table. We specialize in traditional Thakali Khana Sets while celebrating Nepal's diverse cuisine like dhido, sel roti, and Nepali khaja sets.

Our journey began with a passion for preserving ancestral recipes through time-honored techniques. Using fresh ingredients and traditional methods, we create meals that honor Nepal's culinary heritage.

Join us to experience true Nepali hospitality through food that connects generations and communities.
