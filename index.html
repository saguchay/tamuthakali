Our Story

Thakali Khana Set restaurant brings the authentic flavors of Nepal's Thak Khola region to your table. Our culinary journey began with a passion for preserving the traditional recipes passed down through generations of Thakali people.

We carefully prepare each dish using time-honored techniques and the freshest ingredients to create a dining experience that reflects the cultural heritage and hospitality of Nepal. Our signature Thakali Khana Set is a balanced meal that nourishes both body and soul.

At our restaurant, we believe that food is more than sustenance - it's a celebration of culture, community, and the natural bounty of the Himalayas.
